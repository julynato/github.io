About

Senior Energy Consultant with a PhD in Chemical Engineering and 5+ years of post-PhD experience at the intersection of process integration, digital energy tools and industrial decarbonisation.

Formerly Research Director and Board Member at APIL. Currently independent consultant. Elected Director, AIChE Fuels & Petrochemical Division. Visiting Researcher, University of Manchester.

Grounding decarbonisation decisions in engineering reality.

My work focuses on high-level energy systems analysis: whole-systems modelling, heat integration, utility system optimisation and techno-economic assessment. I provide the conceptual foundations and strategic roadmaps that guide informed decisions in energy-intensive industries.

Client-facing lead for international industry partnerships across Europe, South America, India and North America, including collaborations with INERCO and other multinational project teams.

Expertise
01
Energy Systems Analysis and Pinch Analysis
Thermodynamic assessment of industrial processes: energy targeting, pinch analysis, and heat and material balance development to identify efficiency gaps and recovery potential.
02
Utility System Optimisation
Conceptual design and optimisation of process utility systems, evaluating steam, power and fuel configurations including technology selection and multiperiod operational strategy.
03
Industrial Energy Transition Strategy
Optimisation-based frameworks for industrial energy transition, balancing economic and environmental performance to define cost-effective low-carbon pathways from current to future system designs.
04
Digital Energy Tools and Digital Twins
Scoping and technical leadership of industrial digital twin development, integrating process simulation, data-driven models and cloud architecture for continuous energy performance monitoring.
05
Techno-Economic Assessment
Conceptual economic evaluation of industrial energy projects, providing the analytical foundation for investment prioritisation and decarbonisation planning.
06
Technical Advisory and Project Leadership
End-to-end management of energy consultancy engagements, from scoping and proposal development through to client delivery across multilingual, multinational project teams.
Selected Projects

European Crude Oil Refinery — Digital Energy Twin
Energy baseline assessment, pinch analysis and HEN optimisation for a crude distillation unit. Technical lead on development of a SaaS-based digital twin integrating process simulation, ANN models and Azure cloud connectivity for continuous energy performance monitoring.
Active · Refining 2021–2022
Shell — Utility System Optimisation
Multi-objective optimisation of a complex multi-site utility system in collaboration with Shell and the University of Manchester. Analysis of decarbonisation pathways including electrification, renewables integration, CCS and biomass across multiple geographic and regulatory scenarios.
Completed · Industry Collaboration 2022
Shell — LNG Decarbonisation
Decarbonisation strategy analysis for LNG operations in collaboration with Shell and the University of Manchester. Application of optimisation-based frameworks to evaluate low-carbon technology alternatives and transition pathways.
Completed · Industry Collaboration 2024–2025
Next Generation Energy Software Platform
